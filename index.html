Key

1-4 Players Race to collect all of their treasure in the maze.
They may move along any connected path.

First one to collect all of their treasure and return to the
start wins.

Each Turn Players can shift a single row or column in the
labyrinth changing the path's in which players can move.

This includes the position's of all the treasures and player's
within them.
